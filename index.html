Our Features

As one of the main departments of the University, library has always provided an important role for our students and teachers.

Massive collection of books

For decades, we have collected more than 200,000 volumes of various books, more than 10,000 volumes of atlases, more than 50,000 volumes of audio-visual products, and an electronic knowledge base beyond your imagination. .

We are proud of our long history

With the establishment of the school, we have been there for over 40 years to this day .

Fully equipped, both modern and classic

Our library has both modern network facilities, information systems, and computers, as well as unparalleled classic buildings, making the perfect combination of classical beauty and modernity .
